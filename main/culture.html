Culture

Buddhism is one of Japan's two major religions, having been a central force in its culture, art, and governance since the 6th century. It coexists syncretically with the indigenous Shinto faith, a relationship often summarized by the saying: "born Shinto, married Christian, die Buddhist".

Japanese kimono are the traditional dress of Japan. Vintage Japanese women's kimono (kimonos is also an acceptable pluralization) wrap around the body, secured with an obi sash. Kimono for women create a fashion statement that will be remembered!
